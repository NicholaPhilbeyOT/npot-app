Parent / Carer Questionnaire
Please complete before your child's first appointment — takes about 10 minutes
📋 Your answers help Nichola prepare thoroughly for your child's assessment. There are no right or wrong answers — please write in your own words.

This questionnaire helps Nichola understand your child's background, your concerns, and what you hope OT will achieve. The more detail you can give, the better prepared she will be.

Fields marked * are required. Everything else is optional but helpful. Your answers are confidential and stored securely.

🔷 How to set this up in Microsoft Forms

Go to forms.microsoft.com → New Form → copy each question below. Set the form to "Anyone can respond" so parents don't need a Microsoft account. Share the link in your welcome email or as a QR code on your intake letter.

💡 Use Sections in Microsoft Forms to match the 8 sections below — this makes it less overwhelming on a phone screen.
